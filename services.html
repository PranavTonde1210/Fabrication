01

Structural Fabrication

Heavy-duty structural fabrication is our core. We design and build steel frameworks, beams, columns, trusses, purlins, and complete structural skeletons for commercial, industrial, and residential construction projects throughout Pune and Maharashtra.

  • ▸ Steel beams, columns & channels
  • ▸ Roof trusses & purlins
  • ▸ Mezzanine floors & platforms
  • ▸ Pre-engineered building components

Sheet Metal Work

Precision sheet metal fabrication covering all processes — shearing, punching, bending, forming, rolling, and welding. We work with MS, SS, GI, and Aluminum sheets across 1mm to 10mm thickness ranges.

  • ▸ Enclosures, boxes & panels
  • ▸ HVAC ducts & cladding
  • ▸ Roofing & wall panels
  • ▸ Tanks & hoppers
