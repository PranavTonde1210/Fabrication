FAQ

Common Questions

How accurate is this estimate?
The calculator provides indicative pricing based on standard material rates and typical fabrication costs. Actual prices may vary based on design complexity, site requirements, and current market rates.
How do I get a confirmed quote?
Contact us via WhatsApp or the contact form with your requirements, drawings (if available), and location. We'll provide a detailed written quotation within 24 hours.
Is GST included in the estimate?
Yes, the grand total shown includes 18% GST. The line-by-line breakdown shows the pre-GST subtotal and GST amount separately.
Do you handle large orders?
Absolutely. We handle both small custom jobs and bulk industrial fabrication orders. Bulk discount pricing is available — contact us to discuss your volume requirements.
